Rules

  • 🔴 You are Red (bottom). Black is the computer. Red moves first.
  • 👆 Tap a piece to select it — valid destinations appear as blue dots. Tap a dot to move.
  • ↗️ Men move one square diagonally forward only.
  • ⚡ Captures are forced. If any jump is available you must take it.
  • 🔁 After a capture, keep jumping with the same piece if further jumps are available — the move ends only when no more jumps are possible.
  • ♛ Reach the far end to be crowned King — kings move in all four diagonal directions.
  • 🏆 Win by capturing all opponent pieces, or leaving them with no legal moves.

Play async with a friend

After every move the URL updates automatically with the complete game state encoded in the fragment.

Click 🔗 Copy link and send it to your opponent. They open the link, see the board exactly as you left it, make their move, then copy the new URL back to you.

The URL is the save file. Bookmark it to resume a game later. No server, no login, no account — just a link.
